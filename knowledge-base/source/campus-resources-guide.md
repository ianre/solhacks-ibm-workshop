# Campus Resources Guide

Workshop note: This document is sample content for the Becas y Recursos workshop. It is meant for demo use only and is not official university policy.

## Section 1: Food pantry

The sample campus food pantry is called the Jaguar Pantry. It is located in the Student Success Center, Room 120, next to the student ID office.

Sample hours:

- Monday to Thursday: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- Friday: 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- Saturday and Sunday: closed

Sample access notes:

- Students can visit once per week
- A student ID is preferred, but staff can still help if a student forgot it
- The pantry offers groceries, snacks, toiletries, and some school supplies
- Pantry visits are described as confidential in this workshop sample

## Section 2: Counseling and mental health support

The sample Counseling Center is in Wellness Hall, Suite 210. Students can schedule appointments by phone at 555-0102 or by using the campus wellness portal.

Sample hours:

- Monday to Friday: 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM

Sample services:

- Short-term counseling
- Crisis appointments
- Group support sessions
- Stress and time-management workshops

After-hours support in this workshop sample is handled through a 24/7 crisis line at 555-0199.

## Section 3: Tutoring and academic support

The sample Tutoring Center is in the Learning Commons, Floor 2. It offers support in math, writing, chemistry, biology, and introductory programming.

Sample hours:

- Monday to Thursday: 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM
- Friday: 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM

Sample access notes:

- Drop-in tutoring is available for math and writing
- Appointment-based tutoring is recommended for science and programming courses
- Students can book through the Learning Commons desk at 555-0144

## Section 4: Student health services

The sample Student Health Clinic is in Health Services Building, Room 101. Students can call 555-0118 for appointments.

Sample hours:

- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM

Sample services:

- Basic sick visits
- Vaccinations
- Health education
- Referrals for outside care

This workshop sample says basic visits are included in student fees, while labs and prescriptions may cost extra.

## Section 5: Emergency aid

The sample Office of Student Support manages emergency micro-grants for urgent needs such as housing instability, transportation, textbooks, utilities, and food emergencies.

Sample process:

1. Complete the online emergency aid form
2. Upload any requested documents
3. Meet briefly with a student support case manager if asked
4. Wait for a decision email

Sample response time: two to five business days for routine requests, faster for documented emergencies.
